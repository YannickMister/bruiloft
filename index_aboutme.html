About me

My name is Yannick Mister. I'm an aspiring (mainly Geo-related) coder. I love Annick.

My bachelors study started in 2012, at the

Wageningen University and Research Centre

. Living far below sea level, and close to water, hydrology always had my interest. Therefore I studied

Soil, Water, Atmosphere

. Soon enough, during this study, I came in contact with

GIS

, and this opened a whole new world to me. Being able to create maps myself, choosing what data to show, and how, triggered something in me; I started following more courses on the subject, and even started teached for a while. By the time I finished my bachelors, and now I am proud to say that I am studying for a Master of Science degree in Geo-Information Sciences. And I love it!
